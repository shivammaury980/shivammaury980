    About Me

    Greetings! I am Shivam Maurya, a first-year BCA student at Sage University, Indore, Madhya Pradesh, and I am proud to be powered by Sunstone Eduversity.

    Introduction

    I am passionate about coding, ethical hacking, and web development. This passion for technology and the digital world has led me to create this website, which serves as a platform for practicing coding and sharing my experiences in the field of technology. Throughout this journey, I have been guided by the knowledgeable and inspiring Arpita Tiwari Ma'am, who has been instrumental in shaping my understanding of these domains.

    Educational Background

    My educational journey has been a stepping stone to where I am today. I completed my 10th and 12th grades at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh. The years 2020-21 and 2022-23 marked my academic milestones, and the knowledge and skills I acquired during these years have been instrumental in shaping my path in the world of technology.

    Life in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh, India

    Fatehpur, a serene city in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, India, is where I call home. This city's rich cultural heritage and the warmth of its people have played a significant role in shaping my character and values. The city's vibrant atmosphere has always encouraged me to explore the ever-evolving world of technology.

    Why This Website?

    The creation of this website serves multiple purposes in my journey. Here are some of the key reasons:

    1. Coding Practice

      As a BCA student, coding is an essential part of my curriculum. This website acts as a virtual playground where I can experiment with various coding projects, implement what I learn in my courses, and showcase my coding skills to the world.

    2. Sharing Knowledge

      I believe in the power of sharing knowledge. Through blog posts, tutorials, and articles, I aim to impart valuable insights and information related to coding, ethical hacking, and web development. It's my way of giving back to the community and helping others on their learning journeys.

    3. Guided by Arpita Tiwari Ma'am

      Arpita Tiwari Ma'am has been a mentor and guide who has consistently inspired me to explore the depths of technology. With her guidance, I've learned to think critically, solve complex problems, and stay updated with the latest trends in the tech industry.

    Conclusion

    My journey in the world of technology is just beginning, and I'm excited to see where it leads. I'm committed to continuous learning and growth, and I invite you to join me on this exciting voyage. Feel free to explore my website, read my articles, and reach out if you have any questions, feedback, or if you simply want to connect.
